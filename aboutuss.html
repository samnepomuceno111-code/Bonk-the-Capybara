Bonk-the-Capybara Tips:

  • Aim carefully! ONLY bonk the capybara to earn Bonk Points.
  • AVOID the bombs at all costs. One wrong bonk will cost you points.
  • Stay ALERT. As your Bonk Points increase, the chaos speeds up.
  • You only have 60 seconds. Every click counts.
  • Focus on accuracy over speed. Random clicking might hurt your score.
  • The higher your score, the harder it gets. Do not panic.
